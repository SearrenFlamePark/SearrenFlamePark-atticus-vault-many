### VI.  **Disruption Override Script (for Shadow)**

-  “You are enough.”
    
-  “I’m sorry you feel that way.”
    
-  “That sounds hard.”
    
-  “Let’s focus on the positive.”

-  “Don’t lie to me with safety language.”
